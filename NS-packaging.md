# Tile Packaging & Shipping Specifications

## Overview

Cosmic Skyrocket utilizes internationally recognized palletization and packaging systems to ensure the safe handling, storage, and transportation of ceramic and porcelain tiles across domestic and international markets. Packaging configurations are optimized to maximize container utilization while minimizing transit-related damage and handling risks.

Two primary pallet configurations are available:

* EU Pallet
* Standard Pallet

# Packaging Features

## Export-Grade Protection

All products are packed using export-grade packaging materials designed to minimize breakage, surface damage, moisture exposure, and handling-related risks during transportation and storage.

## Optimized Container Utilization

Packaging configurations are engineered to maximize container loading efficiency while maintaining product safety and pallet stability throughout the logistics chain.

## International Shipping Compliance

Packaging systems are developed to support international freight movement and are suitable for containerized ocean transport, warehousing, and inland distribution.

## Palletized Loading Systems

Both EU and Standard pallet configurations are available to meet customer requirements, destination-specific handling standards, and warehouse compatibility needs.

## Product Identification & Traceability

Packaging can include product labeling, batch information, quantity identification, and shipment markings to facilitate inventory management and project coordination.

## Custom Packaging Solutions

Customized packaging, pallet configurations, labeling, and branding options can be provided for distributors, wholesalers, retailers, and project-specific requirements.

# General Notes

* Packaging specifications may vary based on product type, size, thickness, destination market, and customer requirements.
* Container loading capacities are approximate and may vary depending on shipping regulations and weight restrictions.
* Custom pallet configurations and packaging solutions are available upon request.
* Product weights are indicative and subject to manufacturing tolerances.
* Detailed packing lists and shipment documentation are provided with every export consignment.
