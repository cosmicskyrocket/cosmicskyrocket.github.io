Lascaux

Inspired by the ancient cave paintings of Lascaux — humanity's first great artistic statement — this tile captures organic earthy pigments and a prehistoric surface texture.

Lascaux's primordial surface quality and rich ochre and sienna tones bring a deep human warmth and powerful cultural resonance to contemporary interiors and hospitality environments.

Lascaux is designed for contemporary architecture where subtle texture, depth, and material-inspired surfaces are used to shape a refined spatial experience. It adapts well to living areas, offices, hospitality interiors, bathrooms, and commercial settings where understated detail and modern styling need to feel deliberate and sophisticated.

The collection's surface character pairs easily with concrete, metal, wood, and soft neutral palettes, making it highly flexible for layered interior schemes. Its modern proportions and finish options help create clean visual flow across floors and walls while keeping the final result practical, durable, and visually composed.
