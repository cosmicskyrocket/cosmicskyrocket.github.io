Aesthetica Wilde: Specification-Led Guide for Interior Tile Design

Porcelain Performance, Styling, and Lifecycle Planning for Modern Interiors

Aesthetica Wilde is positioned as a premium marble-look tile option for design teams that want timeless stone expression with the practical advantages of modern porcelain manufacturing. It is a strong choice for spaces that require visual sophistication, repeatable quality, and easier maintenance planning.

At specification level, porcelain tile performance is commonly evaluated through recognized standards such as ISO 13006 and EN 14411. Porcelain categories are generally associated with water absorption up to 0.5%, which supports moisture resistance, dimensional stability, and broad suitability across interior applications.

Design Planning Checklist

  • Build a sample panel with grout options before final signoff.
  • Coordinate tone movement with lighting temperature and room scale.
  • Use larger modules to reduce visual breaks in premium living zones.
  • Map transition pieces early for stair, threshold, and skirting zones.

Performance Planning Checklist

  • Validate wear class against expected footfall and use profile.
  • Use slip-aware surfaces for bathrooms and moisture-prone transition areas.
  • Specify cleaning chemistry that protects finish and grout performance.
  • Set a replacement/wastage allowance before procurement closure.
