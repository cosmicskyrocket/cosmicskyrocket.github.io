Natural Stone Export Packaging Standards

  • Heat-treated wooden pallets
  • Fumigated export pallets
  • Timber crates
  • Plastic strapping and corner protection
  • Stretch wrapping and moisture-resistant protective sheets
  • Container load securing systems

Container loading: Natural stone products are generally shipped in 20 FT containers with typical payload ranges of 24-27 metric tons, depending on product type, thickness, and packaging configuration.

Our Standards

Why Our Packaging
Standards Matter

Proper packaging plays a critical role in protecting tile and natural stone surfaces, minimising breakage, and maintaining product quality during transportation. At Cosmic Skyrocket, we follow strict packaging practices to ensure durability, safety, and reliable global delivery for every shipment.

Protects surface finish from scratches and impact during transit
Minimises breakage and product loss for large international shipments
Moisture-resistant materials prevent damage from humidity and condensation
Optimised container packing reduces shipping costs per sq.m.
