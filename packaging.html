Our Standards

Why Our Packaging
Standards Matter

Proper packaging plays a critical role in protecting tile surfaces, minimising breakage, and maintaining product quality during transportation. At Cosmic Skyrocket, we follow strict packaging practices to ensure durability, safety, and reliable global delivery for every shipment.

Protects surface finish from scratches and impact during transit
Minimises breakage and product loss for large international shipments
Moisture-resistant materials prevent damage from humidity and condensation
Optimised container packing reduces shipping costs per sq.m.
Consistent quality packaging builds trust with global buyers
Suitable for sea freight, air freight, and multimodal logistics
