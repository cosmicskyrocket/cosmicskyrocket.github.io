Who We Are

Transforming Spaces with Premium Materials

Cosmic Skyrocket is a globally trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium natural stones and tiles, delivering high-performance architectural materials to projects worldwide. Our integrated approach to sourcing, manufacturing, quality assurance, and international distribution enables us to consistently meet the requirements of architects, developers, contractors, distributors, and procurement professionals across diverse markets.

Founded in Rajasthan, India, and backed by more than 28 years of industry expertise, we have established a robust network of quarrying operations, manufacturing facilities, and global supply partnerships. Today, our products support residential, commercial, hospitality, and infrastructure projects in over 26 countries, reflecting our commitment to quality, reliability, and operational excellence.

Premium Natural Stones solutions manufactured from carefully selected sandstone, limestone, and specialty stone reserves, supported by integrated quarrying, mining, and processing operations in Rajasthan, India.

Comprehensive porcelain and ceramic tile collections encompassing Italian Marble, Moroccan, Wooden, Material, and stone-inspired series, manufactured to meet the requirements of modern architectural and construction projects worldwide.

Integrated export management capabilities encompassing quality assurance, packaging, documentation, containerization, and international logistics through India's major maritime gateways, ensuring efficient global delivery.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of excellence in the quarrying, manufacturing, and global distribution of Natural Stones and tile products through disciplined operational practices, internationally aligned quality systems, technological advancement, and responsible business conduct, while creating sustainable long-term value for customers, business partners, employees, and other stakeholders across global markets.

Maintain rigorous quality assurance standards, product consistency, and operational excellence across all stages of quarrying, manufacturing, inspection, packaging, and distribution.
Continuously invest in advanced manufacturing technologies, process optimization, innovation, and workforce development to meet evolving industry requirements and customer expectations.
Ensure reliable global supply chain performance through efficient logistics management, export compliance, timely order fulfillment, and responsive customer support.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a globally respected and trusted leader in the Natural Stones and tile industry, distinguished by manufacturing excellence, operational reliability, sustainable growth, technological innovation, and an enduring commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and responsible business practices.

Strengthen our position as a preferred manufacturing and export partner for distributors, developers, architects, contractors, consultants, and infrastructure stakeholders across international markets.
Advance responsible quarrying practices, efficient resource utilization, sustainable manufacturing processes, and environmentally conscious business operations.
Expand our international presence through strategic market development, long-term business partnerships, and continued investment in manufacturing and supply chain capabilities.
What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions from sourcing to delivery, tailored to elevate your architectural vision.

Integrated Quarrying & Manufacturing

Leveraging integrated quarrying, processing, and manufacturing operations, Cosmic Skyrocket produces premium Natural Stones and tile solutions that meet stringent quality, performance, and aesthetic requirements for residential, commercial, hospitality, and infrastructure applications.

Custom Manufacturing & Project Solutions

We provide customized manufacturing solutions tailored to project-specific requirements, including bespoke dimensions, thicknesses, surface finishes, edge profiles, textures, and packaging configurations, enabling architects, designers, developers, and contractors to achieve precise design objectives.

International Export & Supply Chain Management

Our comprehensive export management capabilities encompass documentation, quality assurance, packaging, containerization, regulatory compliance, logistics coordination, and international shipment execution, ensuring efficient and reliable delivery across global markets.

Quality Assurance & Compliance

Stringent quality control procedures are implemented throughout quarrying, manufacturing, inspection, packaging, and dispatch operations. Our products are manufactured in accordance with internationally recognized quality standards to ensure consistency, durability, dimensional accuracy, and compliance with customer specifications.

Sustainable & Responsible Production

We are committed to responsible quarrying and sustainable manufacturing practices through efficient resource utilization, environmental stewardship, waste reduction initiatives, and continuous process improvements designed to support long-term operational sustainability.

Technical Consultation & Client Support

Our team provides dedicated support throughout the project lifecycle, including product selection, technical guidance, specification assistance, order management, and post-sales coordination, ensuring a seamless experience for customers, distributors, architects, and project stakeholders worldwide.
